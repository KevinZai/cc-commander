## AI Search vs. Traditional SEO

This domain covers both:

**Traditional SEO** (`seo-optimizer`, `serp-analyzer`, `backlink-audit`) — targets Google's crawlers and ranking algorithms.

**AI SEO** (`ai-seo`, `aaio`) — targets LLM citation systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude). Different signals, different structured data, different robots.txt directives.

Both matter. Most sites are optimized for neither.
